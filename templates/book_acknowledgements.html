With Thanks

The compilation of this directory would not have been possible without the assistance and support of numerous individuals and institutions who generously shared their knowledge, collections, and resources.

Collectors and Dealers

My sincere gratitude to the many collectors who allowed me to examine and photograph their prized possessions. The dedication of these enthusiasts to preserving golf history has made this work possible.

Auction Houses

Thanks to the auction houses that have shared their records and expertise:

  • Bonhams1793 — For access to historical sales records and ongoing support
  • Mullock Jones — For their specialist golf auctions and market insights
  • Golden Age Golf Auctions — For American market data and expertise
  • Christie's and Sotheby's — For historical auction catalogues

Golf Clubs and Institutions

Special thanks to the historic golf clubs that opened their archives and collections:

  • The Royal and Ancient Golf Club of St Andrews
  • Carnoustie Golf Club
  • The Royal Burgess Golfing Society
  • Los Angeles Golf Country Club
  • British Golf Museum

Research and Documentation

The following publications and archives were invaluable:

  • The Story of the Golf Ball — My previous work, which laid the foundation
  • Golf Illustrated and other period publications from the 1890s-1900s
  • Patent Office records from the UK and USA
  • The H.B. Wood Collection — A legendary collection extensively documented

Personal Thanks

To my wife Paddy, for her patience and support during the four years of research that went into this work. To my family for their encouragement, and to all the collectors who have shared their passion for these remarkable artifacts of golf history.

Every effort has been made to locate and to credit copyright holders of images reproduced in this publication. Apologies are rendered in respect of any omissions.

Kevin W. McGimpsey
Chester, England
December 2023
